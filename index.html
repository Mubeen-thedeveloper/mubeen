Custom Price Calculator — No App Required

Client

Floorstreet (UK)

Project Type

Custom Shopify Coding

Date

April 2024

Fully custom price calculator for Floorstreet's product page — built without any third-party apps using Liquid, JSON, and JavaScript. The calculator handles two modes: area-based calculation (with optional wastage toggle) and quantity-based calculation.

Two Calculation Modes

  • Area-based: Enter area → auto-calculates packs, quantity, price, and wastage
  • Quantity-based: Enter quantity → reverses the math to determine area and pricing
  • Built entirely from scratch in Liquid + JSON + JavaScript

This project demonstrates the flexibility of custom Shopify development — building complex business logic directly into the storefront without relying on app subscriptions.
